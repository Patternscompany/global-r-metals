About Us

Global R Metals is an international – based group, which is a leading participant in the field of buying precious Rare Earth Metals and advanced high value materials. It is also a part of the Authority of Auctioneers group and we are capable of meeting the requirements of the most demanding auction in terms of safety and incorporating a high level of flexibility to existing international clients and technological changes. We have always chosen an independent system in Auctioning market to provide visibility and a unique cost control to deliver proper knowledge and unmatched responsiveness in adaptability, evolution of its products and integration of an international Auctioning rates.

Global R Metals is deeply involved in rare metals recycling. It was presented as the prestigious Marketplace Excellence Award of the ongoing dedication to high business standards, Ethics, Integrity, Honesty, Transparency and Excellent Customer Service.

It means a lot to be recognized for integrity in our business field, as buying in this industry is very competitive. Global R Metals has a strong belief in encouraging trust and knowledge in its customer due to which it has been a big success and we believe that this would continue to be the prime focus.
