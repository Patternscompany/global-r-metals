Thermal Energy


Thermal generation is the process of generating electricity from heat. Heat energy that is turned into electricity can be made in many ways. It can be produced by burning fuels such as coal, oil, gas or wood. It can also be taken from steam from a geothermal field, or created by nuclear reactions.

Global R Metals is currently focusing on thermal Energy's products such as low-temperature biomass dryer which creates free energy. Where biomass is already in use as a fuel source, biomass energy can completely eliminate the need to use non-renewable fossil fuels.

These products are successful in a wide range of industries and have an exceptional long term track record delivering:

  • Substantial energy savings of 10% - 30%
  • Fascinating financial returns with profit of 1 - 5 years
  • Better product quality
  • Reduced carbon emissions
  • More reliable operating conditions
  • Improved production productivity
  • Durability and reliability (15+ years)
  • Superior water efficiency
  • Lower conservation costs
